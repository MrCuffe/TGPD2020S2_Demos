Set in the medieval ages in an alternate fantasy world of mythical creatures and magic, Pokemon live.
These once blissful villages now have an ongoing war resulted from miscommunication within Pokemon.
The Pokemon are now in hope for a hero to settle a truce within their kind.
That hero is you!
You must travel along these villages battling opposing Pokemon.
With the aid of Pokemon and magical healing potions you will conquer.
When winning these battles, you have the opportunity to capture defeated Pokemon. They will accompany you on your journey through battles and visiting the enchanted forests.
